Love Me Not

Congratulations, you and a friend have picked a magical rose! Unfortunately, this rose has a curse. The conditions are much like the childrens game "They love me, they love me not", but with a dark twist. This rose will curse the player who picks its very last petal - and that player will be doomed to never find love, while the winner will find eternal love. You do not know how many petals the rose has. You will take turns picking as many petals as you have risked before the game ends and one of you will be alone forever. Each turn, you will have one chance to claim the amount of petals you will pick. Once you click the rose, the amount of petals you have risked will be picked.

Good Luck!
